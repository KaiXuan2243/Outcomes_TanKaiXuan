Describe the importance of pixel.

Pixel also can call dot or picture element, it is represented by dot or square on computer screen. When we want to let the computer display the image and graphic, pixel is necessarily. Because it is a smallest unit of digital image or graphic, it can combined to something that can see on the computer display and this is why pixel called the picture element. Besides that, pixel also can affect the quality of image and graphic in some situation. First is the quality is depending on graphic card and display monitor. For example, a display resolution of 1280 x 768 of computer can produce maximum 983040 of pixel. Every pixel has a unique logical address, size is 8 bit or more and it has the ability to display millions of different colour in the high-end display device. And the last is the resolution spread of pixel also decide to the quality of display, more pixel per inch of monitor screen will produce the better image.
